What is Online Proctoring?

Seeing and Hearing
the Test Taker

We capture the webcam feed, screen and audio feed of the test taker throughout the entire exam. The session is recorded and monitored by a live proctor.

Knowing Who
the Test Taker is

Each test taker is taken through multiple forms of ID verification, including an ID check and challenge questions before they can take an exam.

Advanced Technology
and Cost-effective

In addition to a live monitor, we also track the test takers facial engagement, IP address and other factors throughout the exam. Each test has an individualized report on any suspicious incident. Initial pricing per exam is $10.

Auditorium Features

Webcam, Screen and Audio Recorded

Throughout the exam, the webcam feed is recorded & monitored by a live proctor, screen is recorded and captured, and audio is captured and recorded.

ID Check & Challenge Questions

Automated ID Check and optional challenge questions before the test starts to prove identity.

LMS/Test Delivery System Agnostic

The student can launch an exam via any LMS system with minimal hurdle. The professor can set up the exam within his normal workflow.

Live Proctor

Our highly trained and experienced proctors can intervene immmedietely in case of an infraction or customer service need. All reports are immediately sent to the professor or administrator.

Incident Reporting

After each test, a full report of all activity is sent to the professor or test administrator, with infractions and suspicious activity highlighted.

Webcam Monitoring

Webcam monitoring of motion, face, environmental analysis (look for moving background).

Audio Monitoring

Audio monitoring of abnormal noise, voice recognition.

Data Security

We are compliant with all the regulatory standards for data security in an education setting. All of our live test monitors are trained and undergo background checks before being allowed to monitor live test takers.

Cost effective

We aim to be the most technically advanced solution on the market, enabling us to keep costs low without sacrificing security. Current pricing is $10/test.

About Auditorium

Auditorium is an online exam monitoring service for institutions looking for a flexible, technically robust remote exam monitoring solution. Our mission is to increase access to online exams, making the experience convenient, cost effective and easy for both the educator and test taker while maintaining integrity. The service offers the flexibility of a self-service/on-demand experience with the security features of live proctoring.

Auditorium is a partnership between HigherNext Inc, and ProctorU, Inc.
