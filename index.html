Seeing and Hearing
the Test Taker

We capture the webcam feed, screen and audio feed of the test taker throughout the entire exam. The session is recorded and monitored by a live proctor.

Knowing Who
the Test Taker is

Each test taker is taken through multiple forms of ID verification, including an ID check and challenge questions before they can take an exam.

Advanced Technology
and Cost-effective

In addition to a live monitor, we also track the test takers facial engagement, IP address and other factors throughout the exam. Each test has an individualized report on any suspicious incident. Initial pricing per exam is $10.
